About Us!

We started this project based on the idea of the average college student. I myself was in college and always used to-do-list. The applications for these list never seemed to include all the functionalities that I wanted. Therefore, I resorted to using paper and categorizing them. Along the way I picked up some techniques to be more productive,time efficient, and make better list. The application you see here is the way I think and manage things. I hope you will enjoy it and it help you as well!
