Features of OnTask!


  • Serves as an integrated to-do-list, time managment, and organization tool.
  • Organize your task by category,priority,time estimate, or dates by simpily clicking on the top of the table.
  • See your time allocations visually by totals for the month from the list, and by categories from the list.
  • Able to edit,delete, or complete task easily.

How to use OnTask efficiently!


  • OnTask is set up on a monthly time tracking basis.
  • Anything on the list that is more than a month away is seen as a long term goal and doesn't belong on the list.
  • This is set up to improve your efficiency and focus on upcoming events.
  • Other than that use the sorting, organization, and features to your advantage.
